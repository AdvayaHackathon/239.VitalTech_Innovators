About Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is a progressive loss of kidney function over months or years that affects millions worldwide. It develops silently with symptoms appearing only at advanced stages.

Early detection is crucial for effective management and better outcomes. Our platform helps identify risks before symptoms appear.

Did you know? Up to 90% of people with CKD are unaware they have it until significant damage occurs.

Progressive Disease

CKD typically worsens gradually, making early intervention critical

Silent Symptoms

Often shows no symptoms until kidney function is severely impaired

Preventable

Early intervention can slow progression and improve outcomes
