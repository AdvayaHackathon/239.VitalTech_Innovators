🏃 Lifestyle Changes & 🩺 Medical Care Tips

🏃 Lifestyle Changes

🟢 CKD Stage 1

  • 🥗 Eat fruits & veggies
  • 💧 Stay well hydrated
  • 🚶‍♂ Daily exercise 30 min
  • 🚭 Quit smoking/alcohol
  • 🧂 Reduce salt intake

🟡 CKD Stage 2

  • 🍽 Moderate protein diet
  • 🥛 Limit phosphorus foods
  • 🥔 Monitor potassium levels
  • 🧘‍♀ Stress management
  • 🏃 Healthy weight

🟠 CKD Stage 3

  • 🥩 Low protein meals
  • 🍚 Lower salt/phosphorus
  • 🛌 Sleep well
  • 💊 Follow medications strictly

🔴 CKD Stage 4

  • 🍗 Very restricted protein
  • 🥛 Strict mineral control
  • 💊 Treat anemia properly
  • 🧑‍⚕ Prepare for dialysis

⚫ CKD Stage 5

  • 💉 Start dialysis treatments
  • 🥬 Strict diet guidelines
  • 🚰 Control fluids properly
  • 🧠 Focus on emotional support

🩺 Medical Care Tips

🟢 CKD Stage 1

  • 🩺 Monitor BP and blood sugar
  • 🧂 Low salt diet
  • 🛑 Avoid unnecessary medicines
  • 🍎 Heart-healthy eating

🟡 CKD Stage 2

  • 📈 Track eGFR regularly
  • 💊 Use ACE/ARB if required
  • 🥑 Control potassium levels
  • 🧠 Educate about CKD risks

🟠 CKD Stage 3

  • 📉 Maintain BP control
  • 🩸 Treat anemia
  • 🥛 Take phosphorus binders
  • 🛑 Tight blood sugar control

🔴 CKD Stage 4

  • 🔄 Early dialysis planning
  • 🩸 Calcium and Vitamin D focus
  • 🚫 Avoid toxic drugs
  • 🧘 Mental health therapy

⚫ CKD Stage 5

  • 💉 Regular dialysis sessions
  • 💊 Proper medicine intake
  • 🛑 Strict food and fluid control
  • 🧑‍⚕ Frequent medical checkups
